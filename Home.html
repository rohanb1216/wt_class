About

Tea Terrace offers a tranquil family stay on a quaint suburban hillside of Wayanad amid terraced tea gardens. With the majestic
Kurichiyar hills above, and a gorgeous valley below, Tea Terrace is uniquely beautiful. Tea Terrace has great accommodation comprising
well-furnished spacious cottages. The place also offers great amenities for entertaining guests of all ages.
